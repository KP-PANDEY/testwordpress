PLACEMENT CELL

The Placement Cell is a student initiative which has evolved from the Intership Cell and it comprised entirely of students. The main aim of the cell is to provide placements & internships to students.

Who we are

The PLACEMENT CELL of S K Somaiya is a link between the students and companies. We help our students to find the right job and companies to find the right employee for their company. We align the requirements of the students and companies giving our students a kick start to their career. The PLACEMENT CELL was originally started as an Internship Cell of the students to help each other find internships through a common platform. The success of the Internship Cell led to the birth of PLACEMENT CELL and today it provides internships and placements to students from all streams of the college. It is a cell run by students who get a leadership experience along with their education.In addition to this we also provide training to our students to excel in their career.

What we do

As the PLACEMENT CELL, we are responsible for placing the students from all streams. Our college offers undergraduate courses in Arts, Science and Commerce. Our college offers BCOM, BA, BSc IT, BSC CS, BAF, BBI, BFM, BMS & BMM. We invite the companies by contacting them through mail, telephone and personal meets. We also provide training to our students to increase their employability by arranging sessions for aptitude, soft skills through various trainers and institutes.

Why us

There are many jobs across the country with the economy getting better and MNCs exploring the nation. Why, then, would someone need placement guidance? The process of recruitment seems simple enough u2013 study, graduate, apply, and get a job. Well, truth be told, it is much more difficult than this. There are plenty of jobs, of course, but there is also stringent competition to match the availability. Plus, many people lose out on jobs even though they are qualified because they do not have knowledge about how the system actually works.
